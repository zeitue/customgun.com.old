About Us



Todd Ramirez Has 30 years experience in all phases of gunsmithing.  He graduated the Colorado School Of Trades in 1978.  He apprenticed under James Palmer Moore.  Todd is Italian trained and has over 9 years experience working on Beretta's and other fine Italian Guns.  He started riflemaking back in the late 70's and has been improving his techniques to make his rifles one of a kind show pieces.  Todd's passion is to make all of his custom work a reflection and personalize it for the person it is built for.  Restoration and Stock Building is a passion of Todd's.
