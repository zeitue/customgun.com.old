Gun Fitting

 

The Custom Shop Offers Four Methods of Gun Fitting.  The method used depends on the customer's needs.  We can fit your gun to you so that if feels like it was custom made for you.

 

  • For Left Hand Shooters
  • We can convert any  right hand gun to a left hand gun for you.
  • Inletting - flawless inletting with results close to 1/4"
  • Wood Removal - this method does require stock refinishing.
  • Hot Oil Bending
  • New Stock to your Dimensions
  • Dimensions can be taken off a stock that fits you now.
  • Dimensions of previous fitting
  • The most preferred way is a personal fitting. 

 

Personal Fitting - Includes one on one consultation.  I check for length of pull, pitch, cast and drop.  Length of Pull will be adjusted with pitch.  Castoff at heal and toe will be adjusted.  Then after those procedures are done we will shoot it on the pattern board.  After we shoot on the pattern board then the drop will be adjusted per customers point of impact desires.
