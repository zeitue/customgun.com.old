Shotguns

 

Todd is a Shotgun Specialist.  Including Nine Years of Italian Training on Beretta Guns and other Italian Guns.  He works on a lot of High End Premium Shotguns, which parts are not available.  Todd specializes in hand making those parts and refitting back to original specs.  This includes heat treating.

 

Cleaning and Maintenance should be once a year depending on the gun, maker, and amount of shooting by the customer.
